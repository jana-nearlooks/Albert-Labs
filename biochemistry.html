Overview

Our Biochemistry Department is a third generation laboratory, headed by an experienced doctoral fellow, backed by highly qualified and motivated team of Senior Technical staff. We use the state-of-art technologies such as CLIA technology, Biochip assay and HPLC technologies for greater accuracy. It is well equipped with latest technology, to perform not only the routine biochemical tests for patients but also a wide variety of super specialized investigations such as Tumor Markers, Therapeutic Drug Monitoring including Cyclosporin, Immunoglobulins, Immunofixation Electrophoresis, Osmolality, Iron Deficiency Panel, Infertility Panel, Hormonal Assays, Stone Analysis, Diabetic Profile and many more.

What We Do?

At Albert Lab, we specialize in designing and conducting comprehensive biochemistry tests that illuminate the fundamental aspects of life at the molecular level. Our mission is to advance scientific understanding through rigorous experimentation and analysis.
